# Research Task: UAV-Mounted GPR for Landmine, Buried Object, and Tunnel Detection

Conduct a comprehensive technical and commercial research study on the feasibility of using **Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) mounted on UAVs/drones** for the detection of:

1. Landmines (metallic and non-metallic)
2. Buried explosives and IEDs
3. Underground tunnels
4. Buried utilities (pipes, cables)
5. Buried objects and unexploded ordnance (UXO)
6. Underground cavities and voids

The goal is to determine whether a startup/company can develop a practical AI-powered drone-based system capable of detecting and classifying underground threats and structures.
